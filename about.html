About me

Anita Gautama is a Graphic Designer and Children’s Book Illustrator. She studied Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College and began illustrating children’s books and short horror stories on Fiverr and Etsy during her studies. With a penchant for anything whimsical and macabre, Anita has built a unique brand for herself, with an art style that extends through many genres. Regardless of the project, her brand of creativity shines through every project she takes on.

You can find out more about her works on her Services page below.
